## 2.1 Account Creation and Registration

### 2.1.1 Account Types

ZenithEdu supports four user account types, each with specific privileges:

1. **Administrator (Admin)** - System management and configuration
2. **Teacher/Faculty** - Academic management and student interaction
3. **Student** - Learning resources and academic information
4. **Parent** - Child monitoring and communication

### 2.1.2 Registration Process

**For Administrators:**
Administrator accounts are typically created during system installation by the technical team. If you need admin access:

1. Contact your IT department or system administrator
2. Provide your institutional email and role justification
3. Your account will be created with appropriate privileges
4. You will receive login credentials via secure email

**For Teachers:**
Teacher accounts are created by administrators:

1. Administrator will create your account using your institutional email
2. You will receive a welcome email with temporary credentials
3. Follow the link in the email to set your permanent password
4. Complete your profile information

**For Students:**
Student accounts are created during enrollment:

1. During admission, your account is created by the admin office
2. You will receive a welcome email with login instructions
3. Your default username is typically your roll number or email
4. Set your password using the provided link

**For Parents:**
Parent accounts are linked to student records:

1. Provide your email during your child's admission
2. An account will be created and linked to your child's record
3. You will receive login credentials via email
4. Use the same credentials to monitor all your children

### 2.2.2 Login Steps

1. **Enter your Email Address:**
   - Type your registered email in the email field
   - Ensure there are no spaces before or after

2. **Enter your Password:**
   - Type your password in the password field
   - Click the eye icon (👁️) to show/hide password
   - Passwords are case-sensitive

3. **Remember Me (Optional):**
   - Check "Remember me" to stay logged in for 30 days
   - Only use on personal devices
   - Do not use on shared/public computers

4. **Click "Sign In":**
   - Click the blue "SIGN IN" button
   - Wait for authentication (usually 1-2 seconds)

5. **Dashboard Access:**
   - Upon successful login, you'll be redirected to your role-specific dashboard
   - First-time users may see a setup wizard

### 2.2.3 Guest Access (Limited)

Some institutions allow limited guest access:
- View public notices and announcements
- Access general information pages
- Cannot access personal data or academic information
- Cannot perform any write operations

To use guest access:
1. Click "Continue as Guest" on the login page
2. Browse available public content
3. Note: Guest sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity

### 2.2.4 Common Login Issues

**Issue: "Invalid credentials" error**
- **Solution:** Check email and password for typos
- Ensure Caps Lock is off
- Try using the "Forgot Password" link

**Issue: "Account locked" message**
- **Solution:** Contact administrator after 5 failed attempts
- Wait 15 minutes for automatic unlock

**Issue: Page not loading**
- **Solution:** Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

**Issue: Session expired**
- **Solution:** Re-enter your credentials
- Check "Remember me" for longer sessions

### 4.1.2 Adding a New Student (Admin)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Student Management:**
   - Click **Users** in the sidebar
   - Select **Students** from the submenu
   - Click the **"+ Add Student"** button

2. **Enter Personal Information:**
   - **Full Name:** Enter student's complete name
   - **Roll Number:** Auto-generated or manually enter (e.g., CS2024001)
   - **Email:** Student's institutional email address
   - **Department:** Select from dropdown (CSE, ECE, Mech, etc.)
   - **Phone Number:** Optional contact number

3. **Academic Details:**
   - **Batch/Year:** Admission year
   - **Program:** Degree program (B.Tech, M.Tech, etc.)
   - **Section:** Class section if applicable

4. **Create Account:**
   - System automatically creates user account
   - Temporary password generated
   - Welcome email sent automatically

5. **Review and Confirm:**
   - Verify all details
   - Click **"Create Student"**
   - Success notification appears

### 4.1.3 Viewing and Editing Student Records

**For Administrators:**

1. Navigate to **Users → Students**
2. Use the search bar to find specific students
3. Filter by department, batch, or status
4. Click on a student's name to view details
5. Click **Edit** to modify information
6. Save changes

**For Teachers:**

1. Navigate to **Academics → My Students**
2. View students enrolled in your courses
3. Click on student name to see academic details
4. Limited edit permissions (attendance, grades only)

### 4.1.4 Student Self-Service Portal

**Students can:**

1. **View Profile:**
   - Navigate to **Settings → Profile**
   - View personal and academic details
   - See enrollment date, department, CGPA

2. **Update Profile:**
   - Edit contact information
   - Upload profile photo
   - Update bio/about section
   - Cannot edit roll number or department (contact admin)

3. **View Academic History:**
   - Check all enrolled courses
   - View grades and CGPA progression
   - See attendance records
   - Review assignment submissions

## 6.2 Login Problems

### 6.2.1 Cannot Access Login Page

**Symptoms:**
- Blank page
- Error messages
- Page keeps loading

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Internet Connection:**
   - Verify WiFi/cellular data is on
   - Try accessing other websites
   - Restart router if needed

2. **Clear Browser Cache:**
   - Chrome: `Ctrl + Shift + Delete` → Select "Cached images" → Clear
   - Firefox: `Ctrl + Shift + Delete` → Clear cache
   - Reload the page

3. **Try Different Browser:**
   - Use Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari
   - Avoid Internet Explorer

4. **Check URL:**
   - Verify correct website address
   - Use https:// not http://

### 6.2.2 Login Credentials Not Working

**Symptoms:**
- "Invalid credentials" error
- Cannot remember password
- Account not recognized

**Solutions:**

1. **Verify Email:**
   - Check for typos
   - Use institutional email provided
   - No spaces before/after

2. **Check Password:**
   - Ensure Caps Lock is OFF
   - Check Num Lock for numbers
   - Try typing password in notepad to verify

3. **Reset Password:**
   - Click "Forgot Password" on login page
   - Enter registered email
   - Check inbox (and spam folder)
   - Click reset link within 1 hour
   - Create new password
   - Sign in with new password

4. **Account Locked:**
   - Wait 15 minutes after 5 failed attempts
   - Contact administrator if still locked

5. **Account Doesn't Exist:**
   - Contact your administrator
   - Provide your details
   - Request account verification

## 7.4 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: Can I use ZenithEdu on my mobile phone?**
A: Yes, ZenithEdu is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. Use any modern web browser.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Email changes require administrator approval. Contact your admin with the request and reason.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, ZenithEdu uses industry-standard encryption and security practices. All data is stored securely and backed up regularly.

### Student Questions

**Q: Can I submit assignments after the due date?**
A: Late submission depends on teacher settings. Some allow late submissions with penalties, others don't. Check assignment details.

**Q: How do I check my attendance?**
A: Navigate to Academics → My Attendance. View summary by course and detailed day-by-day records.

**Q: Can I download my grades?**
A: Yes, go to Academics → My Grades and use the Export or Download option to get a PDF or CSV file.

**Q: How do I book an appointment with a teacher?**
A: Go to Appointments → Book New Appointment, select the teacher, choose an available time slot, and submit the request.

### Teacher Questions

**Q: Can I grade assignments offline?**
A: Currently, grading must be done online through the system. Download submissions if you need to review offline, then enter grades online.

**Q: How do I mark attendance for multiple classes?**
A: Select the course, date, and use the quick mark options like "Mark All Present" or select individual statuses.

**Q: Can students see grades before I publish them?**
A: No, grades are only visible after you save them. Use "Save Draft" to keep grades private while working.

### Parent Questions

**Q: Can I monitor multiple children?**
A: Yes, if all children are registered with the same parent email, you can switch between them in the Parent Portal.

**Q: How do I pay fees online?**
A: Go to Fees → My Children's Fees, select the fees to pay, choose payment method, and complete the transaction.

**Q: Can I communicate directly with teachers?**
A: Yes, use the Appointments feature to book meetings, or check if your institution has enabled messaging features.
